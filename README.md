# IBM Cybersecurity Analyst Professional Certificate

This repository tracks my learning journey through the **IBM Cybersecurity Analyst Professional Certificate**.

I created this repo to keep myself accountable, organise what I’m learning, and build a clear record of my progress as I develop towards an entry-level cybersecurity role.

This is not just a place for copied notes. The aim is to write things in my own words, reflect on what I understand, identify what I still need to improve, and slowly build evidence of consistent learning.

## Why I’m Doing This

I’m using this certificate to build a stronger foundation in cybersecurity and to explore which areas of the field fit me best.

The certificate covers topics such as:

- Cybersecurity careers and skills
- Cybersecurity fundamentals
- Tools and cyberattacks
- Operating systems
- Networks and network security
- Databases and vulnerabilities
- Cybersecurity architecture
- Compliance, standards, and regulations
- Penetration testing
- Threat hunting
- Incident response
- Digital forensics
- Security+ preparation
- Generative AI in cybersecurity
- Job search, CV, and interview preparation

My goal is to treat this as more than just completing a course. I want to understand the field properly, build practical knowledge, and document my growth as I go.
